Content

Home page

Welcome to the Reincarnated as a Slime Universe! Dive into a world where magic, monsters, and mythical realms collide. Reincarnated as a Slime follows the story of a regular guy reborn in a fantastical universe as a humble slime—a creature typically weak and unremarkable. But in this world, he isn’t just any slime. Known as Rimuru Tempest, he gains incredible powers, allies, and a kingdom of his own. Join us as we explore Rimuru’s incredible journey from an unassuming creature to a legendary ruler who impacts the world in ways he never could have imagined! Discover more about The World and meet Characters from every corner of Rimuru’s adventure.

Characters

Reincarnated as a Slime brings together a colorful cast of characters, each with unique abilities, backgrounds, and personalities. From faithful allies to fearsome rivals, each character shapes Rimuru’s world in distinct ways.

Rimuru Tempest: Our protagonist, a slime with unique abilities that allow him to absorb powers, heal allies, and even transform into a human form. Rimuru is a compassionate yet powerful ruler whose strength and wisdom help him build a prosperous kingdom.

Great Sage and Veldora Tempest: Rimuru’s trusted inner voices and guides. Veldora is a mighty dragon Rimuru befriends early in his journey, while Great Sage is an inner presence providing him with vital insights.

Benimaru, Shion, and the Kijin: Fierce warriors who serve Rimuru with loyalty. Each has incredible power and a distinct personality that adds to the strength and unity of the Tempest Kingdom.

Milim Nava and Other Demon Lords: Rimuru’s rival and friend, Milim is one of the oldest and most powerful demon lords. The other demon lords, each with their ambitions and alliances, create a balance of power that Rimuru must navigate carefully.

As Rimuru’s influence spreads, so too does his network of friends and foes. Each character he encounters leaves a mark, enriching the story with their presence and impact.

World

Explore a Land of Mystery and Magic

The world of Reincarnated as a Slime is a place filled with diverse regions, magical beings, and countless mysteries. From the Forest of Jura—an expansive woodland that serves as Rimuru's home base and kingdom—to the terrifying Demon Continent and the prestigious Dwarven Kingdom, each region brims with adventure.

Forest of Jura: A vast, enchanted forest where Rimuru first awakens. Home to goblins, direwolves, lizardmen, and other creatures, this forest becomes the foundation of his future kingdom.

Dwarven Kingdom: Renowned for its master craftsmen and warriors, this realm is where Rimuru forms crucial alliances, gaining allies who help him shape his vision of peace and unity.

Demon Continent and Holy Empire of Lubelius: Opposing powers in the world, with demon lords and holy warriors who have their own agendas. Rimuru encounters powerful figures from each faction, shaping the course of the world.

The world is as much a character in Rimuru's journey as the people he meets, constantly evolving with every alliance and every battle.
