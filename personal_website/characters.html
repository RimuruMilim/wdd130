Main Characters:

Rimuru

Rimuru Tempest is the main protagonist of the series. Initially reincarnated as a slime, he gains the power to devour and absorb others' abilities, allowing him to become a powerful leader and build a new world.

Milim Nava

Milim Nava is a powerful Demon Lord with immense strength and a carefree personality. Despite her destructive power, she becomes one of Rimuru's allies and a trusted friend.

Benimaru

Benimaru is a high-ranking ogre warrior and Rimuru’s general. He is known for his fierce loyalty and exceptional combat skills, often leading Rimuru's forces in battle.

Soei

Soei is a skilled ninja and one of Rimuru's trusted subordinates. He specializes in stealth, espionage, and intelligence gathering, making him an invaluable asset to Rimuru.

Shion

Shion is a loyal ogre warrior with a bubbly personality and unmatched strength. Despite her clumsiness, she is one of Rimuru’s closest allies and a formidable fighter.
